Ensuring Confidentiality in a Canadian Pharmacy

Whenever a customer visits or browses through our website, we collect and process specific information to ensure the company’s safety and maintain security at all times. Likewise, personal data is required to identify the customer when they enter our website and to process their order. The personal information used for these purposes includes the customer's name, shipping address, phone number, and other basic details.

Our staff carefully and diligently secure all payment information provided by the customer. Although the entire payment process is handled by a third party, no financial records are stored for further processing.

Whenever an order is placed at Canadian Pharmacy, it comes with a guarantee of discreet packaging. Our policies prohibit placing any pharmacy labels on the package or parcel, respecting and valuing our customers' privacy.

Additionally, we do not send unwanted notifications to our customers' mailboxes. However, we highly recommend signing up for our regular newsletters to stay informed about the pharmacy’s latest promotions, value deals, and discounts. Our company policies prohibit advertising anything that is not approved or accepted by our consumers.
